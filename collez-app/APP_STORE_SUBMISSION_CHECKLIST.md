# COLLEZ App Store Submission Checklist (Phase 4B.2)

## App Listing Copy

### App Name
COLLEZ

### Subtitle
Student OS for college productivity

### Promotional Text
Stay consistent, rank up, and compete with your college friends in one app.

### Description
COLLEZ is the student operating system built for college life.

Use it to organize your academics, maintain streaks, and compete on leaderboards with classmates and friends.

Core features:
- Timetable, tasks, notes, and PDF vault
- XP, streaks, rank tiers, and leaderboards
- Friends, compare profiles, and challenges
- Live events: trivia, puzzle rush, treasure hunts, and battles
- Coordinator system and college-level engagement

COLLEZ starts with India-first college communities and is designed for fast, low-friction student workflows.

## Reviewer Notes Template

- Login methods: Google Sign-In and Apple Sign-In.
- No paid content required for core functionality during review.
- Test account can be created directly using either login method.
- If event list appears empty, this is expected when no live campaign is scheduled.
- App requires internet for cloud sync, rankings, and social features.

## Compliance Checks
- Sign in with Apple is present and parity maintained with Google sign in.
- Terms and Privacy accessible in-app.
- Push notifications are user-toggleable in Settings.
- No sensitive student documents are uploaded without explicit user action.

## Submission Steps
1. Finalize metadata + screenshots in App Store Connect.
2. Upload iOS build from EAS.
3. Fill App Privacy nutrition labels.
4. Add reviewer notes and submit for review.
